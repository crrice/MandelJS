MandelJS

A from-scratch Mandelbrot explorer, drawn pixel by pixel on a canvas (z ↦ z² + c). Drag a box to select a region — nudge it, scroll to resize — then hit zoom to dive in. back steps out one dive, reset returns to the full view.
